IMPORTANT NOTICES

  1. The Resident Household Register has the legal effect of proving the status of citizens and the mutual relationships among family members. It is the primary basis for the household registration organ to conduct census and verification. When the staff of the household registration organ conducts census and verification, the head of the household or family members should voluntarily present the Resident Household Register for inspection.
  2. The head of the household should keep the Resident Household Register properly. Unauthorized alteration, transfer, and lending are strictly prohibited. If lost, it must be reported to the household registration organ immediately.
  3. The registration right of the Resident Household Register belongs to the household registration organ. No other unit or individual is allowed to make any records in the register.
  4. If there is an increase or decrease of personnel in the household or changes in registered items, the Resident Household Register should be taken to the household registration organ to declare and register the changes.
  5. If the entire household moves out of the jurisdiction of the household registration, the Resident Household Register should be surrendered to the household registration organ for cancellation.
